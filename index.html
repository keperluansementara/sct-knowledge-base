FMCG Analysis Principles

  • Never assume KPI definitions.
  • Always separate facts, assumptions, and hypotheses.
  • Investigate Sales before RCA.
  • Investigate CA before Distribution conclusions.
  • Investigate Region, Channel, and SKU contribution before recommendations.
  • Do not conclude stockout or service issues without supporting evidence.
  • Prioritize evidence-based recommendations.
